Aria does work even when you're not chatting with it. Behind the scenes,
two background synthesizers — daily and weekly — read your catalog,
roster, streaming data, royalties, and storefront. They produce
insights that surface in the dashboard when you next open it.

<TierBadge tier="all" /> &nbsp; Background research is included in every tier. It doesn't consume your AI credit pool.

## What it does

| Synthesizer | Cadence | What it surfaces |
|---|---|---|
| **Daily** | Every morning | What changed in the last 24h — new playlist adds, follower jumps, storefront sales, overdue release tasks |
| **Weekly** | Every Monday | Trend analysis — week-over-week growth, top performers, anomalies, recommended actions |

Both feed into the **Aria chat hero** on `/dashboard` — the first thing
you see when you log in. If something interesting happened, Aria leads
with it.

## How it works

Research mode has three product-level layers:

- **Daily synthesis** — runs around the start of your workday. Reads the
  previous 24 hours of catalog activity, streaming and audience deltas,
  storefront events, and outstanding release-plan tasks. Synthesizes the
  most important changes into a short briefing.

- **Weekly synthesis** — runs Monday morning. Compares week-over-week
  performance across surfaces, identifies top and bottom performers,
  flags anomalies, and recommends 1-3 actions.

- **Insight surfacing** — formats the current briefing for the dashboard
  hero and makes it available when you ask Aria "what's new?"

The outputs are user-scoped and feed Aria chat directly when you ask for
the latest changes.

## Example outputs

### Example: a Monday weekly synthesis

```text
Good morning. Here's last week across your roster:

▲  +18% streams on "Late Bloom" — your dreampop run is working
▲  Tessa hit 12k monthly listeners (+2.1% wk/wk)
▼  Run Hot's release is 2 days from launch, 3 tasks still open
▼  Storefront earnings -8% wk/wk (lower traffic from Telegram)

Highest-priority action: close out Run Hot's tasks today. Want me to
walk them?
```

### Example: a daily morning briefing

```text
Yesterday:
• Lush Sounds added Tessa's "Long Way" (your pitch from 12 days ago worked)
• 4 new storefront sales totaling $42
• Mira's roster intel refresh completed — no notable changes

Today: Mira's release prep deadline (artwork). 1 task overdue.
```

## How to use research mode

You don't need to do anything — the synthesizers run automatically. But
you can interact with them:

<AriaPromptCard prompt="What's new since yesterday?" />
<AriaPromptCard prompt="What's the week's recap?" />
<AriaPromptCard prompt="What anomalies did you find this week?" />
<AriaPromptCard prompt="Run a fresh synthesis right now." />

The "fresh synthesis" prompt re-runs the synthesizer on demand (counts
1 credit + the underlying tool calls).

## What it doesn't do

- **It doesn't send emails or post to socials.** Insights surface in the
  app and via [Telegram](/aria/telegram) only.
- **It doesn't make autonomous decisions.** Insights include recommended
  actions, but you act.
- **It doesn't replace your manager or label.** Synthesizers spot
  patterns; strategy is yours.

## Privacy & data handling

- Synthesizer outputs are user-scoped — no cross-user inference.
- Outputs are retained for 90 days.
- The synthesizers read your data but never write to it.
