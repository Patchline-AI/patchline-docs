Streaming insights aggregates everything that's happening on the
streaming side — monthly listeners, top tracks, playlist features,
momentum — across your catalog and roster. The Metrics agent
(`metrics-action-handler.py`) surfaces it.

<TierBadge tier="pro" /> &nbsp; Pro+ for full streaming insights. Free/Starter see basic per-artist numbers in the [intelligence profile](/artists/intelligence-profile).

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do the numbers come from?">
    Soundcharts is the primary source. Spotify Web API supplements for
    a few specific signals. Apple Music for Artists data isn't
    accessible via API — those numbers come through Soundcharts'
    estimate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How accurate are the numbers?">
    Monthly listeners are exact (Spotify-supplied via Soundcharts).
    Daily stream counts are estimates. Growth percentages are reliable
    for established artists, noisier for very-new artists.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get TikTok / YouTube Music data?">
    YouTube Music numbers come through Soundcharts. TikTok music
    discovery data is partial — TikTok doesn't expose track-level
    play counts publicly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
